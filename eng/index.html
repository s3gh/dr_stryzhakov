Stryzhakov Stanislav - obstetrician-gynecologist, gynecological oncologist, doctor of ultrasound diagnostics. He successfully performs both minimally invasive and those requiring a broader approach to surgical interventions, performing operations of any complexity in gynecology. He has many years of experience in the treatment of pathologies such as: infertility, endometriosis, leiomyoma (fibroids) of the uterus, tumors and cysts of the appendages, pathology of the uterine cavity and endometrium, prolapse of the pelvic organs and urinary incontinence in women.

Treatment of various gynecological pathologies is based on the evidence base of world experience. All operations are carried out in accordance with generally recognized international standards.

Dr. Stanislav professional interests include the treatment of infertility, endometriosis, uterine leiomyoma, pathological processes in the uterine cavity, prolapse of the genital organs and surgical correction of urinary incontinence. The doctor successfully performs laparoscopic operations for giant fibroids, tumors of the appendages. In the treatment of endometriosis, the spread of endometriosis along the peritoneum of the small pelvis is maximally excised, it takes good care of the ovarian tissue, removing cysts with the maximum preservation of healthy ovarian tissue. Performs laparoscopic removal of fibromatous nodes on the uterus of various sizes and localizations.

The doctor is a professional in his field, truly loves his job, he approaches each patient with all his heart, which is confirmed by numerous reviews of grateful patients.
